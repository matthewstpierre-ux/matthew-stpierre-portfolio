Potential Feature Add-Ons

Optional · scope-dependent

After studying what's working on competing roofing sites across the region, here are five features worth considering. None are required — the base packages stand on their own. But if any look like the right move, we can scope them in. Pricing depends on complexity, and is quoted before any work begins.

01

Instant Roof Quote Estimator

A guided 4-step form that asks roof type, approximate size, urgency, and contact info — then delivers a ballpark estimate or schedules a callback. Captures qualified leads at 3-4× the rate of a basic contact form.

Why it matters: turns curiosity into a captured lead before they leave the site.
02

Insurance Claim Assistance Page

A dedicated page positioning you as the contractor who helps homeowners navigate storm damage and insurance claims. Major trust signal — and it targets a high-intent search keyword ("[city] roofing storm damage insurance").

Why it matters: high-value, high-intent, low-competition local SEO play.
03

Manufacturer Badge Wall

Trust badges from the suppliers you work with — GAF, IKO, BP Canada, Owens Corning, Malarkey, etc. Real social proof. Converts skeptical homeowners on the spot.

Why it matters: third-party credibility that you can't fake.
04

Live Google Reviews Embed

Pulls 5-star reviews directly from your Google Business Profile onto the homepage, in real time. Updates automatically as new reviews come in. Critical for new businesses scaling trust quickly.

Why it matters: social proof that compounds as you grow.
05

Drone Inspection Booking Widget

Offer a free drone roof inspection bookable directly through the site. Differentiates from competitors, positions you as modern and tech-forward, and is a fantastic top-of-funnel lead magnet.

Why it matters: a hook competitors don't have, generating leads without ad spend.
